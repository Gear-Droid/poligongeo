 Then we’re off to the panda Pablo’s world, where he is desperately trying to survive in a city full o’ sin, drugs and copious amounts of firearms. Pablo is tired of constantly being down under, the time has come to break the usual way of things. Top of the food chain is waiting for him with open arms, money, power and, of course, whores. But nothing’s that easy in this world.
This gig is run by the only and only Warrior Clan, which has never learned how to share with others. No man in a position of power would ever toss a free bone to a stranger; they’d rather fight for it to the death.
WC holds this whole land’s cash inside their muthafuckin' safes, which makes both police chief and mayor no different from sock puppets, but they are *their* sock puppets. Try droppin’ a word, scratch that, a thought, with any discontent towards them and you’ll feed fishes in the dock in half an hour. Long ago they were simple street dealers, and then they grew an additional set o’ balls and turned the whole city into their leash junkies. You either spend all of your working for the hit or slave yourself to them straight away to avoid the middleman. With such a shitload of loyal puppets only a similarly strong clan can stand against them…

Pablo wants to get his own crew together, but he’s going to need some help. Mo’ pandas – mo’ opportunities to grab everything that you lay your goddamn eyes on. And he’s done just that – searched for like-minded homies, ready to risk everything. Meet the team!
Joshua was Pablo’s best friend back in the hood, up until he suddenly found himself at the top. In 2013 he listened to some dork in a pub and bought a couple bitcoins off of him, quickly forgetting about it up until 2021. Getting a fortune out of nowhere got to his head, so without further ado he just dumped everything into LOONA without research. Needless to say, after getting a taste of high-rise life Joshua ain’t going to let things be.
Jimmy n’ Tom are Pablo’s homies. Constantly hanging around bars and stripper rows, they are known by every single pimp round the city central. Jimmy is a graffiti artist with a dream of earning off of Sotheby’s. Tom’s some computer genius from the Darknet that has managed to rob multiple banks and stay off the grid. However, everyone around him understands that even infinite luck might run out some day – he will fuck up one day. He’s dreaming about setting up a proper legal IT company that will focus on helping the downtrodden.
Jonny n’ Jim are Pablo’s cousins. These two fuck hard. They fuck everyone that has money and is gullible enough. Give them a pen and a month later they’ll give yo’ a Lambo. Dem boys can sell anything to anyone. Real professionals.

The crew gets together to band into the strongest clan in history, but what they are facing isn’t some small group, they aren’t enough for this gig. To reach their goal they are going to need at least 2000 pandas from ‘round the world.
It is time to get the game goin’. Every single damn junkie o’ this city will soon know the names of Pablo and his muthafuckin’ crew. Get ready… to join their fight for money and power!
